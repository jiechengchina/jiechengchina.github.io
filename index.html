About Us

Yueqing Jiecheng Tools Co., Ltd. is a professional hardware tools supplier based in Zhejiang, China. Under our brand JIECHENG, we serve customers in international markets and support global online and offline sales channels, including Amazon.

We focus on practical and market-oriented hardware tools, helping customers complete projects efficiently and economically. By combining supply chain advantages, stable production quality and continuous product improvement, we aim to create long-term value for distributors, importers, e-commerce sellers and professional buyers.
